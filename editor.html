Editor's Note

Artificial Intelligence is one of the greatest inventions the human race has created. Its contribution to our society today has a great impact on making our lives much more efficient, but our relations with this tool can vary depending on how dependent we are.

Author's Insights

As for the author, I think that AI is an efficient tool not only for a person’s private use but also for their work. So for a student to use AI for their studies will be a great idea to study efficiently, but if students became dependent on this tool, they could not really learn the competencies they need to accomplish.
