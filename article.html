UNESCO: AI and the 2030 Agenda

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has the potential to address some of the biggest challenges in education today, innovate teaching and learning practices, and accelerate progress towards SDG 4...

UNESCO is committed to supporting Member States to harness the potential of AI technologies while ensuring that its application in educational contexts is guided by the core principles of inclusion and equity.

In conclusion, the promise of “AI for all” must be that everyone can take advantage of the technological revolution under way.

Does AI help college students?

Generative AI gives teachers, students, and administrators access to powerful tools that can be put to great use in education. However, understanding the pros and cons is key to utilizing these tools effectively...

It can streamline administrative tasks, free more time for teachers to build relationships, and tailor students’ learning experiences according to their individual needs.
